Chicken Nuggets

Ingredients

  • 3 skinless, boneless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup Italian seasoned bread crumbs
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tablespoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup butter, melted

Steps to Cook

  1. Microwave chicken nuggets
  2. Eat them
